Registration Task - 02 Deadline: 5th January 2024

Innovation and Research are the pillars of India’s 21st century, contributing majorly to the construction and infrastructure sector. Get the opportunity to contribute your part by assembling all enthusiasts who build models & simulations inspired by problems faced due to natural hazards in our initiatives, Seismic and Cenex.

Task 2 Submission

Task description

  1. You have to devise strategies such that you have to bring a minimum of five teams in the Seismic & Cenex through your contact.
  2. A team that registers on the website will be counted as one participant.

Task Guide:

  1. Talk to your civil professors regarding Seismic & Cenex, and explain Aakaar's initiatives to them about how Aakaar promotes academic and practical knowledge in civil engineering. Request them to encourage their students to participate in these events.
  2. Prepare a 10-minute PPT explaining Cenex & Seismic, and present it among your civil engineering batchmates across all years in their classes by getting permission from your professors.

Submission

  1. Make a Google sheet mentioning the team lead's full name and phone number who has registered for Seismic or Cenex from your reference. Make sure to mention in which event they have registered against every team. You have to submit the link to the Google sheet (make sure to give view access to everyone).

Note

  1. Make sure to tell the team that is registering through you to put your CR ID in referral while registering on the website.
