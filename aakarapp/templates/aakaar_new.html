ABOUT US

Aakaar is the annual festival of the Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Bombay comprising a range of engaging competitions, workshops, lecture series, panel discussions, industrial talks, exhibitions, and various entertaining learning events. Since its inception in 2009, it has become Asia’s largest Departmental College festival. Aakaar serves as a forum for discussion and offers opportunities for developing new skills for engineering students across India.

GOAL OF AAKAAR

Aakaar in its 16th edition is all set to scale new heights with many new events and competitions. Aakaar provides an exceptional platform for aspiring civil engineers to explore and learn about the vastness of the stream. It instigates students to innovate, create and learn by means of interesting events and competitions.

SYMPOSIUM

Aakaar IIT Bombay is proud to launch it's Eigth International Civil Engineering Symposium (ICES) with the prime motive of encouraging young research enthusiasts in the field of civil engineering and will act as a platform to exchange ideas & scientific knowledge through intense professional interaction among aspiring civil engineering researchers. We believe this will lead to the fruitful germination of collaborative research work.

Paper Presentation

The Symposium Paper Presentation is a forum for young and promising students enthusiastic about research to present their work in front of some of the most experienced professors in the country, civil engineering leaders and industrialists.

Prizes Worth: 50k +

Poster Presentation

The Technical Poster Presentation is a way to communicate your research and your understanding of the topic in a concise format. It requires analyzing, evaluating, and synthesizing the idea and creatively demonstrating the findings of your research work in a visually capturing way.

CR PROGRAM

The College Representative Program is the core of Aakaar’s outreach initiative. We at Aakaar hope to connect with college students across India to pique their interest in Civil Engineering and provide a platform to pursue the same. This Program enables you to create a strong network of like-minded people who can support your future professional pursuits while gaining invaluable experience, organising unique events, and putting on spectacular events.

SMART PITCH

Aakaar IIT Bombay, in association with the Society for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (SINE), launches the Smart Pitch competition, a platform that allows all startups to pitch their ideas to an esteemed panel of investors and SINE mentors. Smart Pitch is a competition for Civil Engineering start-ups that offers excellent opportunities. Smart Pitch offers opportunities for startup incubation at SINE, IIT Bombay, as well as investment in startup ideas through our investor panel.
